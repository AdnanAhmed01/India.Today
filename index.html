INDIA

India, officially the Republic of India (Hindi: Bhārat Gaṇarājya), is a country in South Asia. Bhārata-varṣa (Bharata-varsha) is said to be derived from the name of thithankar Rishabhanath's son Bharata. Several Puranas state that it is derived from the name of Bharata, the son of Rishabha.

Agra Fort

The Agra Fort is a historical fort in the city of Agra and also known as Agra's Red Fort. Built by the Mughal emperor Akbar in 1565 and completed in 1573.

Charminar

The Charminar is a monument located in Hyderabad, Telangana, India. Constructed in 1591, the landmark is a symbol of Hyderabad and officially incorporated in the emblem of Telangana.

Qutab Minar

The Qutb Minar, also spelled Qutub Minar and Qutab Minar, is a minaret and "victory tower" that forms part of the Qutb complex, which lies at the site of Delhi's oldest fortified city.

Nahargarh Fort Jaipur

Nahargarh Fort stands on the edge of the Aravalli Hills, overlooking the city of Jaipur in the Indian state of Rajasthan. Along with Amer Fort and Jaigarh Fort, Nahargarh once formed a strong defence ring for the city.
